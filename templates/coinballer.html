Coinballer

Welcome to the game of Coinballer, the big dick swinging gambling game.

In this game, up to 100 players are gambling on who has more resources.

Rules

The rules are simple.

Players can make a bet or raise their bet at any time.

The game ends when 144 bitcoin blocks, approximately 24 hours, have passed without a raise.

The player who has contributed the most money to the pool at the end of the game wins the whole pot, minus a small 2% fee for the host.

Setup

To set up a game, you will need to be familiar with running a program on the command-line or in Terminal, although players will not need this functionality to play.

You can download the command-line client here.

To start a new game, the command is ./coinballer new_game <player 1 address> <player 2 address> ...

For games with large numbers of players, place the addresses in players.txt one per line and run ./coinballer new_game players.txt

This command will provide a URL with the addresses and bets for participants to play with.

To make a bet players must send the appropriate amount of bitcoin to their betting address.
