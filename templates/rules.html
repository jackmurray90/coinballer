Rules

In this game, up to 100 players are gambling that they have more worth.

Players can make a bet or raise their bet at any time.

The game ends when a certain number of bitcoin blocks have passed without a raise that is equal to or exceeds the maximum bet. This length is configurable.

Anybody who has contributed at least as much as the nth highest bet wins an equal share of the pot. n is configurable.

A 2% fee is taken from the pot for the host.

To start a game, you must collect each player's payout address and input it into the new game form.

Bets that are received after the end of the game are automatically sent back to the player's payout address, minus a 0.0001 BTC transaction fee.
